Quick Answers

How to manage multiple IA deadlines?
Break each IA into smaller tasks. Use the Pomodoro timer above. Prioritize and communicate with teachers if overwhelmed.
Feeling stuck with the EE?
Set small daily word count goals. Use the body scan meditation to release tension. You're not alone—connect in the forum.
When to use breathing exercises?
Before exams, during study breaks, or anytime you feel anxious. Even 3 cycles can help.
